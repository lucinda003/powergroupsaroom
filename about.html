History of Barangay Ambonao

Barangay Ambonao in the municipality of Calasiao, in the province of Pangasinan. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 6,879. This represented 6.85% of the total population of Calasiao.

Demographics
The household population of Ambonao in the 2015 Census was 6,209 broken down into 1,499 households or an average of 4.14 members per household.

Population
bThe population of Ambonao grew from 3,347 in 1990 to 6,879 in 2020, an increase of 3,532 people over the course of 30 years. The latest census figures in 2020 denote a positive growth rate of 2.18%, or an increase of 670 people, from the previous population of 6,209 in 2015.
